I am a postdoctoral researcher in the [Department of Statistics & Data Science](https://www.cmu.edu/dietrich/statistics-datascience/index.html) at Carnegie Mellon University, working with Professor [Kathryn Roeder](https://kathrynmroeder.github.io) and Professor [Jing Lei](https://www.stat.cmu.edu/~jinglei/).

I obtained my Ph.D. degree from [Department of Statistics](https://www.stat.uci.edu) at University of California, Irvine in 2024, advised by Professor [Annie Qu](https://faculty.sites.uci.edu/qulab/). Before that, I got Master degree from [University of Illinois Urbana Champaign](https://stat.illinois.edu) in 2019, and Bachelor degree from Tongji University in 2018. 

My research interests include:

- Data integration
    - Problem: multi-task learning, block-missing data, multi-resolution data, semi-supervised data, noisy label
    - Methodology: semiparametric inference, representation learning, latent factor model
    - Application: genomics, mobile health

- Causal inference
    - Problem: individual treatment rule, synthetic data, mediation analysis
    - Methodology: statistical learning, deep learning, optimal transport
    - Application: precision medicine

- Data privacy
    - Problem: dependent data
    - Methodology: latent factor model, distribution-dependent mechanism

- Interdisciplinary studies:
    - gene regulatory network
    - pregnant women sleep and stress
